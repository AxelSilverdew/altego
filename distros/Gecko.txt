**GeckoLinux** is a spin based on the openSUSE distribution line, Leap and Tumbleweed, with focus on polish and out-of-the-box usability on the desktop.
**Notable features**
- Allows you to run a live session (currently impossible on openSUSE.)
- Has a wide range of DEs ranging specific to your choice, drastically reducing ISO size.
- Patented software which is unavailble by default on openSUSE due to restrictions are included.
- All the stabiltity of Leap or sturdy bleeding edge packages of Tumbleweed.
- Special attention has been given to the quality of the font rendering.
**Available DEs**
- Cinnamon
- XFCE
- GNOME
- Plasma
- Mate
- Budgie
- LXQt
