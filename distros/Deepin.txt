**deepin** is a Debian-based distribution that aims to provide an elegant, user-friendly and reliable operating system. Home to the original Deepin Desktop Environment.
**Notable features**
- Deeply integrated Qt 5 and GTK toolkits for consistent integration.
- Available Deepin Apps, which replace and provide extra functionality to their counterparts. 
- Interchangable Debian servers for stable but grounded software repos.
- The quickest and most familiar successor to Windows and Mac users.
**Available DEs**
- DDE
- Others available through PPA's
