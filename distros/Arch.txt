**Arch Linux** is a rolling-release, minimal style system, where the user configures the system to their need.
**Notable features**
- Simplicity, install what you need, no extra bloat.
- Bleeding Edge, the latest and greatest packages, right at your fingertips.
- Keep it simple stupid, no need to overcompicate things.
- Pacman, package manager with most packages.
- AUR, no need to look for packages on shady websites.
- Arch wiki, a vast, easy to use wiki with examples and troubleshooting steps.
