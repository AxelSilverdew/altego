**Microsoft Windows** is a nt-kernel based OS. It is the single most popular Operatingsystem to date, but is plauged with malware and buildt in spying features. 
**Notable features**
- Microsoft Cortana, your own personal voice assistant, free of charge!
- DirectX, a way to render 3d applications, Windows only.
- Support, any problem you face on windows has already been solved or is beeing solved.
**Available Versions**
- Currently supported versions: Vista,7,8/8.1,10
- Pro/Enterprise editions
**Drawbacks**
- Has alot of privacy issues
- A big target for viruses
- Closed source
- Paid
- Forced updates (can be turned off)
