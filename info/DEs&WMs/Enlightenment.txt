**Enlightenment** is a desktop environment which started as a project to build a Window Manager for X11, making it one of the oldest and most prolific to date. Covering a wide array of use-cases, this DE is packed in addition with it's own libraries and applications. It is expected to move to Wayland in due time.
**Notable features**
- EFL Base libs.
- Cross-compatible, to an extent.
- Native base and Tizen applications.
- Feel thrown back to a simpler time.
