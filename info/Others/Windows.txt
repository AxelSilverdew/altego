**Microsoft Windows**, a NT-kernel based OS, it is the single most popular operating system to date, sadly plauged with malware and built in spying features. 
**Notable features**
- Microsoft Cortana, your own personal voice assistant, free of charge!
- DirectX, a way to render 3D applications, Windows only.
- Support, any problem you face on Windows has already been solved or is beeing solved.
**Available Versions**
- Currently supported versions: 7 ,8/8.1 ,10
- Pro/Enterprise editions
**Drawbacks**
- Closed source
- Paid
- Forced updates (can be turned off)
- non-existent privacy
- lots of malware and security issues (200 millions lines of spaghetti code)
- proprietary
- zero respect for user time and sanity
