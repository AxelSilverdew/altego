**ConnochaetOS** (formerly DeLi Linux) is fully free/libre GNU/Linux distro for x86 computers with limited resources, based on Slackware and Salix OS. “Fully free” is the emphasis of this distribution.
**Notable features**
- Follows the KISS principle.
- Contains no proprietary, non-free software, blobs or firmware.
- Easy to jumpstart with the 'slack-n-free' repo.
**Available DEs**
- OpenBox
- Anything Compilable
