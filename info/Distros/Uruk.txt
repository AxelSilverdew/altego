**Uruk** is a free software desktop distribution based on Trisquel. Uruk primarily uses .deb package files, but strives to support a wide range of package formats, including .rpm files.
**Notable features**
- Make Stallman proud, in a stylish way!
**Available DEs**
- MATE
- Anything on PPAs.
