**Tumbleweed** is a Linux based operating system, openSUSE distribution that runs blazingly fast, prevents sitting all day in config, and guarantees easy to use GUI settings.
**Featuring:**
zero-cost distro
rare need of terminal
wider hardware support
traits of SLE with faster updates
patterns, install DEs easily
minimal net-install ISO
Snapper, OBS, Zypper
Yast to rule them all
